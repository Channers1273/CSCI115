Characters - Brok & Sindri

Kratos Atreus Brok & Sindri

The Huldra brothers, Brok and Sindri are two dwarven blacksmiths that serve as the players main source of upgrades for their armour, weapons, and special abilities. They also serve as a good form of comic relief. It was these two who were responsible for forging the Norse god Thor’s hammer, Mjölnir, and also Faye’s Leviathan Axe which would later be given to Kratos.
