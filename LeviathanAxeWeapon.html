Weapons - Leviathan Axe

Leviathan Axe Blades of Chaos
  • Good for close, and mid-range attacks
  • Applies a frost statis effect to enemies

The Leviathan Axe first belonged to Kratos’ wife, Faye. It was made by the Huldra brothers as a way to combat their previous creation, Mjölnir, weilded by the god of thunder, Thor. Faye then passed it on to Kratos before she died. This weapon, which is said to have been “infused with the screams of twenty frost trolls,” serves as the main weapon for Kratos in the Norse realms. It can freeze enemies when thrown at them and features a wide range of frost based attacks.
